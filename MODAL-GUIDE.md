# Guidelines Modal - Quick Reference

## ✅ New Feature: Interactive Guidelines Popup

### How to Use:
1. Click **"View Response Guide"** button on any disaster card
2. Beautiful modal window opens with detailed emergency guidelines
3. Click **"Got It!"** button or **X** to close
4. Can also close by:
   - Clicking outside the modal (on dark overlay)
   - Pressing **ESC** key on keyboard

### What's Inside Each Modal:

#### 🏚️ **EARTHQUAKE Guidelines**
- **Immediate Actions**: DROP, COVER, HOLD ON technique
- **Critical Safety Rules**: What NOT to do during shaking
- **After Earthquake**: Dealing with aftershocks and evacuation

#### 🔥 **FIRE Guidelines**
- **Immediate Actions**: Pull alarm, alert others, evacuate
- **P.A.S.S. Method**: How to use fire extinguisher properly
- **Evacuation Safety**: Door checking, smoke avoidance, route planning
- **After Evacuation**: Assembly points and building re-entry

#### 🌊 **FLOOD Guidelines**
- **Immediate Actions**: Move to higher ground
- **Critical Water Safety**: Never walk/drive through flood water
- **During Flooding**: When trapped and signaling for help
- **After Waters Recede**: Damage assessment and safety checks

#### 🌀 **CYCLONE Guidelines**
- **Before the Storm**: Securing outdoor items, preparation
- **During the Cyclone**: Staying indoors, interior shelter
- **Eye of the Storm**: Critical warning about calm period
- **After the Cyclone**: Checking for damage and hazards

### Visual Features:

**Color-Coded Sections:**
- 🔵 **Blue boxes** = General guidelines and procedures
- 🔴 **Red boxes** = Critical warnings (what NOT to do)
- 🟢 **Green boxes** = Helpful tips and best practices

**Responsive Design:**
- ✅ Full-screen modal on mobile
- ✅ Centered popup on desktop/tablet
- ✅ Scrollable content for long guidelines
- ✅ Touch-optimized close buttons (44px+)

### Technical Details:

**Modal Features:**
- Smooth fade-in animation
- Backdrop blur effect
- Dark mode compatible
- Prevents background scrolling when open
- Fully accessible keyboard navigation

**Content Structure:**
- Organized with headings and sections
- Numbered steps for procedures
- Bullet points for quick tips
- Bold emphasis on critical actions

### Testing the Modal:

1. **Open any disaster module** (Fire, Earthquake, Flood, Cyclone)
2. **Click "View Response Guide"**
3. **Read through the guidelines** - scroll if needed
4. **Notice color-coded sections** for different information types
5. **Try closing methods**:
   - Click "Got It!" button
   - Click X in top-right
   - Click outside modal
   - Press ESC key

All methods work smoothly!
